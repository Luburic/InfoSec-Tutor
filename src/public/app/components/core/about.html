InfoSec Tutor

The InfoSec Tutor is an e-learning application designed as a teaching aid for two courses on the Faculty of Technical Sciences, University of Novi Sad.
Here students can find information, demos and benchamrks of various information security concepts.
In its current version the application offers lessons on cryptography, with a focus on symmetric-key and public-key encryption.
